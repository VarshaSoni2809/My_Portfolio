Position of Responsibility

The term "position of responsibility" refers to a role or position in which an individual is entrusted with specific duties, tasks, or obligations that require them to exercise authority, make decisions, and carry out responsibilities that have an impact on others or an organization. It typically implies a higher level of accountability and requires the individual to act in a responsible and reliable manner.
